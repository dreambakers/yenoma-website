Frequently asked questions

Yenoma is intended for small and medium-sized businesses and also for schools or for private use.

yenoma must not be used for political, religious, military or health purposes.

How long are the contracts?

The minimum contract term is 3 months. When it expires, yenoma will switch back to the free version.

Can I switch plans later?

You can switch to the pro version at any time. After the paid period yenoma switches back to the free version automatically.

Will my data be saved when I change plans?

Of course - you won't lose any data when you switch versions. Please note that only the Pro version supports the export of data.

Can I cancel my account at any time?

Yes, you can cancel both free and pro accounts at any time. Please note that no refunds can be made for Pro accounts.

Is there a setup fee?

No, there are no other costs. Neither for creating many surveys, nor for surveys with many respondents.

What types of payment methods are accepted?

Currently only Paypal is accepted for payments. We are working on the implementation of other means of payment.

Will you sell my data or Email address?

No - we will never sell your contact data or your survey data. And we will not do anything with your survey data except storing it.

Why is yenoma so cheap?

yenoma is new and being cheap is the fastest way to get more users. Additionally we think that online surveys should be affordable to anyone.
