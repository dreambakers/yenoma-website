The yenoma app in more detail

Let us take you on a little tour - and show you what yenoma looks like.

EASIER SURVEY CREATION

It's as easy as 1-2-3 to create your first survey

Add a new survey

Click Add, type in a survey title, enter a question. That's it - you just finished all necessary steps for your first survey.

Save your survey

You can preview exactly what the survey looks like on the respondents side. When you are finished editing hit Create.

Share your survey

After creating your survey it is already online! Now let people know by sharing a link or the provided QR code. It cannot get simpler.

MORE FLEXIBILITY

Different questions require different kind of answers

12+ answer types

yes, no, maybe - thats's just one of many answer types. yenoma also supports multiple choice, star ratings, slider, text, value inputs...

Additional explanation

Need to explain your question a bit more? You can add additional text to make it easier to answer.

Other answer

If none of your answers fit the respondant - ask them what else they would they say.

MORE LANGUAGES

The yenoma user interface supports several different languages

Multiple UI languages

Switching the app's user interface to another language just takes a second - choose between English, German, Spanish and others.

Extensive character set

Ask your questions in almost any language - German umlauts, French accents or even Japanese characters? No problem!

Found a mistake?

Your preferred language is not supported? Or you found a translation mistake? Simply use the internal feedback to let us know!

QUICKER RESULTS

In the end - results are what you're after

Statistics view

No complicated math here - just a useful overview on what was answered.

Data export

You can export (download) an Excel compatible data file with all responses included.

Details view

Want to check a certain response in detail? You can, while you're on the go.
